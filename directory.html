1. Fellowships & Research Programs

AI safety fellowships with stipends, mentorship, and compute. Many programs run multiple cohorts per year across the US and UK.

MATS (ML Alignment Theory Scholars)

12-week in-person research program. $15K stipend + $12K compute + private housing + catered meals. 120 fellows, 100 mentors. Berkeley & London. 5 streams: Empirical, Policy, Theory, Technical Governance, Compute Infrastructure. ~70% receive 6–12 month extensions with continued stipend, housing, and compute.
Summer 2026 (June–Aug) apps closed — Autumn 2026 apps open late April

Anthropic Fellows Program

4-month program. $3,850/week + ~$15K/mo compute. Scalable oversight, interpretability, adversarial robustness. US, UK, or Canada work authorization required. No PhD needed.
July 2026 cohort — rolling applications

Iliad Intensive / Fellowship

Intensive: 1-month math intro to alignment. Travel + housing covered. London. Fellowship: 3 months, $6K–$15K stipend. June–Aug 2026.
April 2026 Intensive closed — check site for future cohorts

Pivotal Research Fellowship

9 weeks in London. £6K–8K stipend + accommodation + compute. ~70% get extensions up to 6 months.
Current cohort (Feb–April 2026) in progress; check for Q3/Autumn 2026 applications

CAIS AI & Society Fellowship

3 months in SF. $25K stipend + travel + meals. Social/policy focus.
Deadline: March 24, 2026

ERA Fellowship (Cambridge)

8 weeks fully funded. Competitive stipend + lodging. Technical safety & governance.
Winter 2026 closed — sign up for next round

SPAR

3-month remote, part-time (5–40 hrs/wk). Compute covered. $7K in Demo Day prizes.
Spring 2026 in progress — check for fall cycle

Second Look Research (UChicago XLab)

Replicate load-bearing AI safety papers. Contact zroe@uchicago.edu for details.
Closed — deadline passed March 15

Global AI Safety Fellowship

Up to 6 months at FAR.AI, CHAI, Conjecture, UK AISI. Up to $30K.
2025 cycle closed — express interest for next iteration

2. Grants & Funding

Fund your own research through grants, regranters, and open RFPs. Many programs offer rolling applications with fast turnaround.

Manifund Regranting

Expert regrantors with $100K+ budgets each. $5K–$50K typical grants. Can fund in under 1 week.
Always open — rolling

Long-Term Future Fund (LTFF)

EA Funds. Wide range: $5K–$120K+. Technical research, policy, infrastructure. 6–8 week response.
Always open — rolling

Schmidt Sciences — Trustworthy AI RFP

Up to $5M per grant (Tier 1: up to $1M, Tier 2: $1M–$5M). 1–3 year projects + compute.
Deadline: May 17, 2026

Survival & Flourishing Fund (SFF)

$20–40M+ total across multiple tracks. Main, Freedom, and Fairness tracks.
Main round: April 22, 2026

Coefficient Giving — Transition Grants

Career transitions into AI safety. Covers study, internships, training. Case-by-case amounts. Global.
Check current deadline on site

Coefficient Giving — Technical AI Safety RFP

$50K–$5M per grant. ~$40M total across 21 research areas.
Closed — applications ended July 15, 2025

UK AISI Alignment Project

Up to £1M per project + cloud compute + AISI team support. £27M+ in Round 1.
Applications currently closed; expected to reopen summer 2026

Foresight Institute AI Nodes

Grants + office/community space in SF and Berlin (Berlin opening April 1, 2026).
Rolling

3. PhD Programs & Academic Fellowships

Multi-year university programs (3–5 yrs) with tuition, stipends, and a focus on original research. Several dedicated AI safety PhD fellowships exist.

Vitalik Buterin PhD Fellowship (FLI)

5-year PhD. Full tuition + $40K/yr stipend + $10K/yr research fund. US/UK/Canada universities. Strongly technical focus; note alignment-first career commitment requirement (AGI-racing company clause).
Next cycle ~November 2026

Cooperative AI PhD Fellowship

Up to $40K/yr for up to 3 years. Cooperative AI and safety topics. 240 applicants in 2026 cohort.
Check website for next cycle

Cooperative AI Research Fellowship (Cape Town)

3 months in Cape Town. $3K/mo + flights, housing, office, meals, compute. Great for junior researchers.
Applications closed — check website for next cycle

Schmidt Sciences AI2050

Multi-year awards for Early Career & Senior Fellows. 2025 cohort: 28 scholars, $18M+ total. Nomination-based only — cannot directly apply.
Nomination-based — no open application

PIBBSS x Iliad Research Residency

6-week residency for math researchers. $6K–$15K. London or remote. Mentorship + post-residency funding support.
Rolling — apply early
